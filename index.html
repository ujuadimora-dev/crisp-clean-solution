❓ 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

A: No, as long as we have secure access. Our team is trained, vetted, and insured.

Q: What’s included in a deep clean?

A: Everything in a standard clean, plus baseboards, appliance interiors, vents, and detailed attention to neglected areas.

Q: Do you bring your own supplies?

A: Yes, we bring all eco-friendly tools and products unless you request otherwise.

Q: How do I schedule or cancel a service?

A: You can call, email, or book online. We appreciate 24 hours’ notice for cancellations.

Q: Are your services pet-friendly?

A: Absolutely! We use non-toxic, safe products and love working in pet homes.
